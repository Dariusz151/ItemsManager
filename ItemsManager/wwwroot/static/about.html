Smart Fridge App.

About

Purpose and usage

  Roughly one third of the food produced in the world for human consumption every year — approximately 1.3 billion tonnes — gets lost or wasted. The smart fridge application has been created to help us not to waste food in our houses. Just by smart use of food products we can decrease this serious problem.
  Smart fridge app allows us storage list of food in our fridge and keep track of expiration dates. Also, by choosing specific items application will return us some example recipes. The recipes database is public, so all users can add own recipes and look out for others. Of course I intend develop this application and add new features soon. Be patient!
